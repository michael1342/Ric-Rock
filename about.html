The Beginning

Ric Rock Mining founded in Jos by CEO Marcus van der Berg with a team of 12 geologists. First gold exploration concession awarded in the Kaduna region.

2003 ‚Äî First Major Discovery

Jos Deep Gold Strike

Ric Rock's geological team identifies a 4.2 million ounce gold deposit in Plateau State, propelling the company to international prominence.

2008 ‚Äî Global Expansion

Entering South America

Acquisition of Chilean copper rights and establishment of Ric Rock Latin America marks the company's first intercontinental operation.
